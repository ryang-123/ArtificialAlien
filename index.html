Artificial Alien

Greetings Earthlings! Welcome to our site!

  • We are Artificial Alien; a 3 piece Hard Rock band from Vaughan, Ontario. Forming in late 2016, Artificial Alien has spent their time creating an extensive catalog of original songs; curating a sound that is uniquely theirs that pulls inspiration from classic rock, alternative rock, punk, and grunge. Since the summer of 2018, the band has become a force for Rock music in Toronto’s live music scene, playing and headlining venues such as Sneaky Dee’s, Lee’s Palace, The Horseshoe Tavern, Cherry Cola’s, etc. Their sound has translated into explosive and unforgettable live shows, with the band actively engaging the audience both on stage and off it, with band members adding to the sweat filled pandemonium by joining the moshpits during performances. Artificial Alien is also affiliated with the Absinthe Pub at York University. In their effort to keep Rock n Roll and live music thriving in the city, the band organises band nights for local musicians looking to gain live performance experience on Saturday nights, providing an inclusive space that welcomes friends and family to join and support their fellow artists.
